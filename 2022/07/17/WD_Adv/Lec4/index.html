Interpolation of the convective fluxes

  • central differencing / linear interpolation
    • may generate oscillatory/unbounded solutions
    • 2nd order accuracy

  • UD (upwind differencing)

    • first order accurate
    • bounded (non-oscillatory) and diffusive

  • SOU LUD BW (2nd order UP; linear UP; Beam-Warming)

    • this scheme is oscillatroy (unbounded) for highly convective flows or in the presence of strong gradients
  • TVD (add flux or gradient limiter)
    • limiter function $\psi(r)$ is based on monitoring the ration of successive gradients

  • TVD + unstructured meshes

    • ration of successive gradients

  • Flux limiter $\psi$ and gradient limiter $\phi$
