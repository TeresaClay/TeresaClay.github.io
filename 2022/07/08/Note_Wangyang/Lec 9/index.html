控制微分方程

标量形式，输运形式, 守恒形

$
\begin{aligned}
\text{连续性方程} \quad \quad & \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}+\nabla \cdot (\rho\boldsymbol{U})=0 \\ \\ \text{动量方程} \quad \quad & \frac{\partial \rho u}{\partial t}+\nabla \cdot (\rho u \boldsymbol{U})=\nabla \cdot (\mu\nabla u)+S_\phi \\ & \frac{\partial \rho v}{\partial t}+\nabla \cdot (\rho v \boldsymbol{U}) =\nabla \cdot (\mu \nabla v)+S_\phi \\ & \frac{\partial \rho w}{\partial t}+\nabla \cdot (\rho w \boldsymbol{U})=\nabla \cdot (\mu \nabla w) + S_\phi \\ \\ \text{湍动能} \quad \quad & \frac{\partial \rho k}{\partial t}+\nabla \cdot (\rho k \boldsymbol{U})= \nabla \cdot (\frac{\mu_t}{\sigma_t}\nabla k)+2\mu_t \boldsymbol{S} _{ij}\cdot \boldsymbol{S}_{ij}-\rho \epsilon \\ \text{湍耗散} \quad \quad & \frac{\partial \rho\epsilon}{\partial t}+\nabla \cdot (\rho\epsilon\boldsymbol{U})=\nabla \cdot (\frac{\mu_t}{\sigma_t }\nabla \epsilon)+C_{1\epsilon} \frac{\epsilon}{k}2\mu_t \boldsymbol{S}_{i,j}\cdot \boldsymbol{S}_{j,i}-C_{2\epsilon}\rho \frac{\epsilon}{k} \\ \\ &\frac{\partial \alpha}{\partial t}+\nabla \cdot (\alpha \boldsymbol{U})=0
\end{aligned}
$

通用微分和积分形式

$\begin{aligned}
\quad \text{微分形式} \quad \quad & \frac{\partial \rho\phi}{\partial t}+\nabla \cdot (\rho\phi\boldsymbol{U})=\nabla \cdot (\mu\nabla \phi)+S_\phi \\ & \color{grey}{\frac{\partial \rho \phi}{\partial t}+\text{div}(\rho\phi\boldsymbol{U})=\text{div}(\mu \text{ grad} \phi)+S_\phi} \\ \\ \text{积分形式} \quad & \int_{CV}\frac{\partial \rho\phi}{\partial t}dV+\int_{CV}\nabla \cdot (\rho\phi\boldsymbol{U})dV=\int_{CV}\nabla \cdot (\mu \nabla \phi)dV+\int_{CV}S_\phi dV
\end{aligned}$

  • $\phi=1,u,v,w,k,\epsilon$

  • 要求解的未知量：$u,v,w$; 已知 $\boldsymbol{U}$

  • Introduction book 3. Chapter

  • 高斯散度定理分析对流项 $ \color{grey}{
    \int_{CV}\nabla \cdot(\rho\phi\boldsymbol{U})dV=\int_S \boldsymbol{n} \cdot (\rho\phi \boldsymbol{U}) dS}
    $
